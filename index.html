Subdivision & Consolidation of Land

For property owners, investors, and developers, subdivision and consolidation are effective ways to unlock the full potential of your land.
Subdivision involves dividing one parcel of land into two or more separate titles which allows you to build multiple dwellings, generate additional rental income, or sell individual lots.
Consolidation (also known as amalgamation) is the process of combining two or more adjoining titles into a single title. This can provide greater flexibility for development, improve land use, or enhance the overall value of the property.
We can guide you through both subdivision and consolidation, helping you navigate the legal requirements and ensuring a smooth process from start to finish.
